App Name Description
PriceAlarm  
PriceAlarm is a utility which lets you set up price drop alerts for items you desire to buy like Electronics, Home Appliances, Household Accessories, Clothing, Tech Gadgets, Video Games & more! Easy-to-use and lightweight, it allows you to create alerts for various online items and notifies you when the price reaches your target price. It is especially useful for keeping a wish-list of items from different stores in one place and keeping track of prices during festive sales like Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Diwali, Christmas on online stores such as Flipkart, BestBuy, Myntra, Target, Argos, Ajio, Gamestop and seasonal sales like summer, spring, autumn, and winter on stores like Playstation, Steam & Xbox!
3x3Puzzle  
3x3Puzzle is a fun logic puzzle where numbered tiles should be arranged in an increasing numerical order by sliding tiles around. Its a perfect puzzle game for kids and adults alike to sharpen their minds as well as improve focus and concentration!
